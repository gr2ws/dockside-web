Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 03, 2025

Hotel Name: Luxury Hotel
Location: Dumaguete City, Philippines

Luxury Hotel values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our property, access our website, or use any of our services.

We collect personal data when you:

  • Make a reservation via our website, phone, email, or in person
  • Check-in or check-out at the hotel
  • Use our amenities or request special services
  • Subscribe to newsletters or promotional materials
  • Participate in surveys, contests, or loyalty programs

The personal data we may collect includes:

  • Full name
  • Address
  • Contact number and email address
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Payment details (credit/debit card, bank info)
  • Flight information
  • Special requests (e.g., dietary needs)

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To manage reservations and booking confirmations
  • To facilitate check-in, stay, and check-out
  • To respond to inquiries and guest requests
  • For billing, payment, and accounting purposes
  • To personalize your guest experience
  • To send updates and promotions (with consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

Your personal data may be shared with:

  • Payment processors and banks
  • Third-party service providers (e.g., transport)
  • Government authorities when required
  • Legal and accounting advisors
  • Affiliates and partners, where necessary

All third parties are bound to maintain confidentiality and security of your data.

Your personal information is securely stored. We retain it only as long as necessary for:

  • The original purpose of collection
  • Legal or regulatory compliance
  • Asserting or defending legal claims

We implement organizational, physical, and technical measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure networks and firewalls
  • Encryption and access control
  • Training and awareness for hotel staff
